Certainly! Here are the necessary instructions for the Tower of Hanoi game:

Objective:

Move all the disks from Tower 1 to Tower 3.

Rules:

You can only move one disk at a time.

A disk can only be placed on top of a larger disk or an empty tower.

Larger disks cannot be placed on top of smaller disks.

Towers:

Towers are numbered 1, 2, and 3.

Disk movement is specified by selecting the tower from which to move a disk and the tower to which the disk should be placed.

How to Play:

Click on the tower from which you want to move a disk.

Click on the tower to which you want to place the selected disk.

Follow the rules to ensure a valid move.

Winning:

The game is won when all the disks are successfully moved to Tower 3 in the correct order, with the largest disk at the bottom.

Additional Controls:

Use the "Start / Restart Game" button to reset the game.

The "Click here to know instructions" button provides a quick reference to the game rules.

Remember, patience and strategic thinking are key to solving the Tower of Hanoi puzzle. Enjoy the game!

Congratulations!
